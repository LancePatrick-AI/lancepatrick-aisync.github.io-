## About This Repository

This repository is the public home for research, simulations, and technical work by Lance Patrick at the intersection of **AI security**, **multi-agent system verification**, and **AI governance architecture**. The work draws on 20+ years of experience in military, national security and business transformation & optimization program management environments. This experience is applied to the novel challenge of securing and optimizing autonomous AI systems.

Research here spans conceptual architecture, formal threat modeling, interactive simulations, and publication-track academic work. Projects are organized in subdirectories as they develop.

---

## Research Areas

- **Multi-agent AI verification** — architectural frameworks for maintaining oversight integrity in autonomous agent systems
- **Second-order attack surfaces** — threat classes that exploit an architecture's own integrity mechanisms as camouflage
- **Byzantine fault tolerance applied to AI oversight** — adapting distributed systems principles to compliance-agent architectures
- **Containment-independent detection** — mechanisms that bound threat persistence without requiring threat identification
- **AI governance and human oversight** — structural approaches to keeping human judgment in the loop under adversarial conditions

## Projects

**Toward a Resilient Verification Architecture for Multi-Agent AI Systems**

A seven-layer resilient verification architecture addressing the single point of failure paradox in compliance-agent architectures and a novel second-order attack class — the compliance agent cloning attack with decoy retention. Includes formal system and adversary models, theorem-style safety and containment guarantees, and an interactive browser-based simulation.

-  Published preprint (Zenodo): [https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.19248890](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.19248890)
-  Interactive simulation 1: [https://lancepatrick-ai.github.io/lancepatrick-aisync.github.io-/MultiAgent_Verification_Simulation.html](https://lancepatrick-ai.github.io/lancepatrick-aisync.github.io-/MultiAgent_Verification_Simulation.html)
